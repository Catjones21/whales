History of The Greywhale:

Scientific Name: Eschrichtus robustus

Whales are believed to have evolved from mesonychids: furry land mammals that lived around the ancient Tethys Sea (now Mediterranean Sea) and parts of the Asian sub-continent, 55-60 million years ago.

Archaeocetes were the first ancient whale-like animals to evolve around 10 million years later.

25-30 million years ago these whales split into the toother (odontocetes) and baleen (mysticetes) whales.

Around 5 million years ago the modern families of whales had become established.
