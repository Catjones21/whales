GREY WHALES

The Grey Whale Migration

Grey Whales are born to migrate. California Grey Whales have one of the longest migrations on the planet. They travel from Mexico and the Artic, which is about 6,000 miles. They hug the coastline and go through Monterey Bay. When migrating thorugh the coast, it is scary because killer whales can strike the grey whales. Killer Whales will strike the mother whale and the calf. However, they can usually escape.
