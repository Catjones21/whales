Where to Go Whale Watching In Monterey

Whales are such majestic and striking creatures. We encourage you to see whales to see how captivating these creatures are. See all different types of whales up close in the ocean in Monterey, California!

The best time to see grey whales in is December-May. Peak times are January and in March.

The best time to see humpback whales is March through November, peak sightings are July and August.

The best time to see killer whales are mid-April through mid-May and late August through october.
